Communication Checklist

After experiencing technical difficulties in your course, please complete the following checklist to help us better assist you.

  1. I have taken screenshots or pictures of the issue.
  2. I have tried conducting troubleshooting steps on my own.
  3. I have contacted my professor about the issue.
  4. The professor was not able to assist me, they recommended contacting the COLTT Help Desk.

Once you have completed the checklist, please contact the COLTT Help Desk with the information you have gathered.
