Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common course questions.

Where do I submit assignments?
Submit your work in Assignments. If you don’t see the assignment, check the module release conditions or confirm the due date in the course calendar.
Where do I find quizzes or exams?
Quizzes and exams are located under Course Tools → Quizzes or within the corresponding unit folder in Content.
I can’t see an item. What should I do?
Make sure you are viewing the correct unit and that the release date has passed. Some items require you to complete earlier content first.

Tip: If you still need help, use the Email Instructor or Ask COLTT Support links on this page.
