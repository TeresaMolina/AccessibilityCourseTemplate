Quick Tools

Common actions for this course.

  • Panopto Home Page Access course recordings and video content. Open Panopto

    Note: Ensure you are signing in via Brightspace SSO.

  • Zoom Join meetings, office hours, and live sessions. Open Zoom

    Note: Ensure you are signing in via SSO (key icon) with the "utrgv".zoom.us domain and not your personal account for attendance purposes.

  • Classlist for Email / Messages View your classmates, instructors, and TAs / Tutors in Tools → Classlist.

    Note: Email will be sent through Outlook. Messages stay inside Brightspace only, and instructors cannot facilitate messages.

  • Class Progress View grades and reports in Class Progress → Class Progress.

    Note: Class Progress is commonly used for student reports if an instructor decides to review whether you have been active in the course.

Some tools may open in a new tab depending on system settings.
